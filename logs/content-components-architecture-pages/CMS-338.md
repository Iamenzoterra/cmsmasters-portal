## What Was Implemented
Created `ADRViewer.tsx` as a `'use client'` component that renders a two-panel layout: a left sidebar (w-72) listing all 22 ADRs grouped by the 7 V2 categories with a search input, and a right panel that displays the selected ADR's full markdown content. Search filters by title and body. Clicking a related ADR link navigates within the viewer.
