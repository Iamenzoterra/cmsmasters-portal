## What Was Implemented
Created `AppCard.tsx` at `apps/command-center/components/AppCard.tsx`. The component accepts a typed `app` prop and renders the app name in bold, a one-line truncated description, and a status dot with label. Status dot colors map to: gray (not-started), blue (in-progress), green (beta), purple (live). The card wraps a Next.js `Link` navigating to `app.href` (caller encodes the `/phases?app=<id>` filter).
